So what is Alphanode?

Alphanode is an inter-school IT symposium organised by NODE(Network of digital enthusiasts). Pariticipants compete in a variety of events like Web Development, Audio-Video editing, Digital imaging, gaming, senior quiz, group discussions and more!

Basic editing, including color enhancement, the use of filters, and cropping of the photograph is acceptable.

Participants need to bring their own cameras and any other accessory such as tripod stand, Laptop etc that they may require.
